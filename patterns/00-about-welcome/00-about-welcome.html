Minnesota State Community and Technical College's Living Styleguide

The style guide primarily acts to establish and maintain consistent design patterns across a project.

It also serves as a communication tool; communicating code and content decisions between the strategists, designers, and developers on a project, communicating content strategy and best practices to stakeholders and content creators, and communicating project progress and design to stakeholders.

This styleguide is based on the PatternLab Atomic Design System.
